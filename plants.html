    A breif about kalimeris

    Kalimeris is tolerant of heat, humidity and drought, so it is great for the south. Kalimeris can be grown in sun or shade in a well-drained soil. The mum-like blooms arrive in late summer and stick around into fall.

    A short breif about alovera

    Aloe vera, sometimes described as a “wonder plant,” is a short-stemmed shrub. Aloe is a genus that contains more than 500 species of flowering succulent plants. Many Aloes occur naturally in North Africa.

    A short breif about lamium

    Lamium plants are a perfect deer resistant groundcover to brighten a partly sunny to semi-shady spot. Vigorous without being invasive, Spotted Dead Nettles create a unique tapestry beneath small trees.
